# Hype–Discipline Pairing

Distilled pattern: keep **radical forecasts** credible by **locking arms with boring engineering** in the same narrative pass.

## When to use

- You are excited about autonomy, speed, or a new default workflow.
- You can already hear the “but it hallucinates” replies.

## Steps

1. **Grant the limitation plainly** — Stochastic, opaque, wrong sometimes. No mysticism.
2. **Name one guardrail you actually use** — Tests-first, review gates, typed boundaries, human approval on writes, etc.
3. **Tie guardrail to the new workflow** — Explain why the old guardrail *morphs* (e.g., tests become the contract for agents).
4. **One sentence on misuse** — What should people *not* automate yet?

## Anti-patterns

- “Just vibe” with no verification story.
- Safety theater (long lists of principles, zero practices).

## Output shape

- Bold claim.
- One paragraph: limits.
- One paragraph: your default verification habit.
- One line: non-goals.
